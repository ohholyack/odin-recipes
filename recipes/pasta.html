Pasta Recipe

Description

Everyone in the world loves to make pasta.

Just follow the instructions and in no time, you'll be sitting down to dinner.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • water

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and put it on high heat.

  2. When water boils, place pasta in water.

  3. Let boil for 45 minutes for optimum mushiness

  4. Strain and eat!
